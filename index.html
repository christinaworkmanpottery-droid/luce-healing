About Christina

I've spent decades exploring the depths of energy work, spiritual consciousness, and the human experience. As a master energy healer, I've dedicated my life to helping people remember their own power — because the truth is, no one can heal you but yourself.

My role is to move energy, clear what's blocking you, and give you the tools to continue that work on your own. I don't create dependency — I create empowerment. Every session is designed to bring you closer to being your own healer.

My journey has taken me through many dimensions of service: personal healing work, spiritual guidance, and ministering to people at life's most tender moments. As an ordained non-denominational minister, I hold a deep reverence for all spiritual traditions and beliefs. I work with people regardless of their faith background, helping them find peace and meaning in their own way.

I'm also a space and land clearer. I understand that our environments hold energy — the weight of past events, emotions, transitions. I help clear and bless spaces so they feel light, peaceful, and aligned again.

At the end of the day, only you can heal yourself. I'm here to show you how.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is energy healing?

Energy healing is a holistic practice that works with the body's natural energy systems to promote relaxation, clarity, emotional release, and overall well-being. It's based on the understanding that everything—including us—is made of energy, and when that energy becomes stuck or imbalanced, we experience stress, illness, or emotional heaviness. Energy work helps restore flow and alignment.

Can distance healing sessions work as well as in-person?

Yes, absolutely. Energy is not limited by location or distance. Many people find distance sessions incredibly powerful and deeply effective. What matters is intention, presence, and openness—not proximity.

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session will begin with a warm conversation to understand your needs, intentions, and any concerns. There's no pressure. Everything is completely tailored to you, your pace, and your comfort. I'll explain what's happening, honor your boundaries, and ensure you feel safe throughout.

Do I need to believe in energy healing for it to work?

You don't need to "believe" in the same way I do. Openness and willingness are what matter most. Many people come skeptical and leave transformed. Your energy work with me is completely non-denominational and respects your own beliefs and worldview.

What about end-of-life support? Do I need to be religious?

No. My ministry and support are non-denominational, meaning they honor all spiritual traditions and beliefs—including no formal religion. Whether you're Christian, Buddhist, Muslim, Jewish, spiritual-but-not-religious, or anything else, I'm here to support you on your terms.

Can you clear a space I rent, or only owned properties?

I can work with any space—rental or owned. For rentals, we simply ensure the clearing is respectful and non-invasive. The energy of a space shifts regardless of ownership, and you'll feel the difference immediately.

How long is a typical session?

Sessions typically range from 60–90 minutes, depending on what you need. End-of-life and grief support may vary. We can discuss timing that works for you.

Is everything I share confidential?

Yes. Everything you share with me is completely confidential. My role is to witness, support, and hold space—not to judge or share your story. Your privacy is sacred.

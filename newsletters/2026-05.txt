May 2026 is a month of deep emotional excavation followed by grounded renewal. The Scorpio Full Moon opens the month by pulling truth to the surface, and the Taurus New Moon two weeks later asks you to plant something real in the soil of what remains. There are no eclipses this month — which is a gift. After a wild spring, May gives us a chance to integrate, ground, and actually *feel* what we have been moving through.

This is a month for slowing down, listening to your body, and letting your nervous system catch up with your life.

---

## ✦ The Major Transits of May 2026

### 🌕 Full Moon in Scorpio — May 1
The month opens with a Scorpio Full Moon — one of the most emotionally potent lunations of the year. Scorpio rules transformation, intimacy, hidden truths, and what we have buried. This Full Moon will illuminate whatever has been festering in the dark — relationships, resentments, secrets you have been keeping from yourself.

Expect intensity. Expect tears. Expect clarity.

**How to work with it:**
- Take a salt bath the night of May 1. Add rosemary or eucalyptus.
- Journal the question: *What am I ready to let die?*
- Avoid making impulsive decisions — let the energy move *through* you, not *as* you.
- Burn what no longer serves (literally or symbolically).

### ♀ Venus enters Taurus — May 5
Venus comes home. After weeks of fiery Aries energy, Venus moves into the sign she rules, and everything softens. Sensuality returns. You will crave good food, slow mornings, beautiful textures, real touch.

**Healing tip:** Spend money on something that genuinely nourishes your senses — fresh flowers, a good piece of fruit, a candle that smells like something you love. Taurus Venus heals through *embodiment*, not concepts.

### ☿ Mercury enters Gemini — May 11
Mercury comes home too. Conversations open up, ideas multiply, and the mind moves quickly. This is a wonderful time to write, teach, learn, share, and connect. Just watch for mental scatter — Gemini Mercury can pull you in ten directions at once.

**Healing tip:** Write things down. Voice memos count. Get the noise out of your head and onto a surface.

### 🌑 New Moon in Taurus — May 16
Two weeks after the Scorpio Full Moon's emotional release, the Taurus New Moon arrives to ask: *now what do you want to build?* Taurus is the builder, the gardener, the one who knows that real things take real time.

This is the most powerful manifestation moment of the month.

**How to work with it:** See the manifestation section below.

### ☉ Sun enters Gemini — May 21
Gemini season begins. The energy shifts from grounded earth to airy curiosity. Conversations matter. Connections matter. Following your interest matters.

### 🌙 Mars in Cancer (all month)
Mars is in its fall in Cancer — meaning the warrior energy gets quieter, more emotional, more protective. You may feel less "go go go" and more "let me protect what I love." That is not weakness. That is wisdom.

**Healing tip:** If you feel less productive than usual, you are not broken. You are honoring a slower current. Do less. Feel more.

### Long-term context: Saturn & Neptune in Aries, Pluto in Aquarius, Uranus in Gemini
The big slow-movers are still reshaping the world. Saturn in Aries is asking us to build new foundations from courage. Neptune in Aries is dissolving old spiritual identities. Pluto in Aquarius continues the long death and rebirth of how humans gather and relate. Uranus in Gemini is rewiring how we communicate, learn, and think.

You are living through a reshuffle. Be gentle with yourself.

## ✦ Manifestation: Working with the Taurus New Moon (May 16)

The Taurus New Moon is *the* manifestation moment of May. Taurus governs the physical world — your body, your money, your home, your sense of safety, your worth. This is not the moon for big chaotic dreams. This is the moon for **rooted, specific, embodied desire**.

**The ritual:**

1. **Get quiet.** The night of May 15 or morning of May 16, sit somewhere you feel safe. No phone.
2. **Touch something real.** Hold a stone, a piece of wood, the floor. Taurus needs *contact*.
3. **Ask your body, not your mind, what it wants.** Not what you *should* want. What does the animal in you actually want to grow into your life over the next six months?
4. **Write three intentions.** Make them specific and physical. Not "abundance" — *"I want $X in my account by November."* Not "love" — *"I want to be touched with tenderness three times a week."*
5. **Plant something.** Literally. A seed, an herb, a bulb. Taurus rituals work best when you involve the earth.
6. **Commit to one small action per week** that moves each intention forward. Taurus does not believe in magic without effort.

The Scorpio/Taurus axis this month is the axis of *death and resurrection* — what you released on May 1 makes room for what you plant on May 16. Trust the cycle.

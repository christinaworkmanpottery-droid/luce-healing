Spiritual Self-Care
Is Not A Luxury
  • 🌿It's not about baths or fancy oils. It's about protecting your energy from what drains you.
  • 🛡️"No" is a complete spiritual sentence. Don't apologize for setting boundaries.
  • 🧘Find 5 minutes of silence. Not to pray, not to fix, just to be human.
  • 📓Check in with your body. Where are you holding tension? That's where you need care.
  • ✨Give yourself permission to do nothing. Your worth isn't tied to your output.

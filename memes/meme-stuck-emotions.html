Where You Hold It
Your body has been keeping the score. Here's where emotions get stuck — and how to free them.
  • 🫀Chest tight? Grief lives here. Place a hand. Sigh out loud.
  • 🦴Tense jaw? Words you didn't say. Hum, yawn, or scream into a pillow.
  • 🌊Heavy belly? Fear and worry settle here. Breathe deep into it.
  • 🪨Stiff hips? Old emotion lives in your hips. Move them slowly, daily.
  • 🔥Tight shoulders? Responsibility you've been carrying. Let it drop.
